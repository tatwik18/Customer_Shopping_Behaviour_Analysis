# 📊 Employee Churn Analysis & Prediction

## 📌 Problem Statement

Organizations are facing challenges in retaining employees. High employee turnover leads to increased hiring costs, reduced productivity, and loss of experienced talent.

This project aims to **analyze employee behavior and proactively identify employees who are at risk of leaving**, enabling organizations to take preventive actions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify key factors contributing to employee attrition
* Analyze employee satisfaction and engagement levels
* Predict employees who are likely to leave (churn prediction)
* Provide actionable insights to improve retention

---

## 🧠 Approach

* Selected a **pilot dataset of employees**
* Performed **data cleaning and preprocessing**
* Conducted **exploratory data analysis (EDA)**
* Built a **Machine Learning model** to predict employee churn
* Extracted **key features influencing attrition**

---

## ❓ Key Analysis Questions

* What is causing employees to leave?
* Who is most likely to leave (high-risk employees)?
* Are employees satisfied with their job?
* Which departments have the highest churn?

---

## 📊 Project Questions

* What does success look like? → **Highly accurate prediction model**
* What does failure look like? → **Inaccurate predictions**
* What trends are important? → **Factors causing employee churn**
* What actions affect the trend? → **HR strategies & retention policies**

---

## ⚙️ Tech Stack

* Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* Machine Learning (Scikit-learn)
* SQL / BigQuery
* Data Visualization (Looker Studio)
* Google Colab

---

## 📈 Key Insights (Example)

* Employees with low satisfaction levels are more likely to leave
* Certain departments show higher attrition rates
* Workload and performance pressure impact churn
* New employees are more prone to leaving
